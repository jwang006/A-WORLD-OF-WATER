A WORLD OF WATER

In the era of freedom and openness, how can we achieve both personality freedom and social equality? When we are in space sorroundings familiar things, we can be ourselves; In a strange environment, we often feel constrained and overwhelmed.

Water is indispensable in our life and exists everywhere we live, like in cities or suburbs, at home or outdoors. In the natural world, water is free; With the development of science and technology, we use our wisdom to use water, change water, and even create water. Through our hands, water is controlled, used, wasted and divorced from nature. We all know that Water is domesticated by humanity, and humanity is also nourished by water. So, how the natural environment influences our life and the sensations? Can the water around us change our attitude towards life? I want to show the influence of water on our lives and what we get from water.
